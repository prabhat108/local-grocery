About BigDay

At Ketramart, we strive to provide women all over the world with an endless selection of affordable gowns and dresses that will make their formal and special occasions unforgettable. We totally understand the throbbing of finding THE perfect dress for your wedding, your party, or other events.
And, with over 8,000 dresses in sizes from 2-26W and in over 50 colors, we've got you covered in silhouettes, styles, and themes that will make you feel beautiful and confident at your or your friend's and family's events. Ketramart, makes the dresses in your wardrobe affordable.

Return Policy

We hope every customer gets what they are expecting! Just in case you are not satisfied with our product, we’ve made the return&refund process super easy. We will issue a full refund of the cost of the returned product(shipping fee excluded)if you are not satisfied with your item(s). Request return approval by contacting our customer service within 14 days of receiving your item(s). The item must be returned in its original condition. How to return Step 1: Please send us an email at support@dressafford-service.com with your Order ID within 14 days upon receipt of your package. The returns without prior approval from our Customer Service are not acceptable. Step 2: Please find the return address and a return form to fill in, and go to a local post office and ship out the item(s) within 7 days upon receiving the return approval.UPS, DHL, and FedEx shipping services are not accepted. Please email us the tracking number of your returned parcel. Step 3: We will process your refund as soon as your package is delivered and inspected at our facility. It takes 8-10 days for banks to process our refund request. Note*: We do not accept the kind of “buy-more-keep-one” shopping. Exceptions All customized items do not qualify for return or exchange, and neither do gifts. All personal care items (including lingerie, swimwear, etc.) are final sale. Due to hygiene issues, there are no returns/exchanges. All returned products must be kept in their original condition. Custom duty charged by your government will not be refunded. Rush fee is non-refundable.

Shipping & Payment

Shipping Area Our website ships to the United States (including Alaska and Hawaii, but excluding U.S. territories). Through our partnership with some reliable couriers, we are pleased to provide an enhanced international shopping experience for our customers in select countries, with support for many more coming soon. For the list of countries where we can ship , please click here. Shipping Cost International shipping is calculated based on a number of factors including the weight of the items, the destination country postal code and the desired shipping method(Standard/Express). To check the shipping cost to your country, add the desired items to cart, fill in your address and select the shipping method, you'll see the shipping cost. We currently offer two shipping methods: Expedited Shipping This shipping method is the fastest available. Delivery times are between 3 - 5 days to all major destinations. Standard Shipping Standard shipping takes a bit longer than expedited shipping but costs less. Delivery times are between 6 - 8 days to all major destinations. Order Tracking Please note that for each shipment notification email you receive, tracking may not be available for the first 24-48 hours. If 48 hours have passed and you are still unable to track your order, please contact us to ask for help. Once you get your tracking number from our email, you could learn the status of your parcel by entering our tracking number on the tracking page of the courier's official website.

We then use this data in aggregate form to identify overall visitor behavior patterns. Our goal in using this information is to improve our site by making it more responsive to the needs and preferences of our users. CLICK-STREAM DATA (AND COOKIE TECHNOLOGY) In order to register on-or purchase products from-Dress Afford websites, your browser must be set to accept "cookies." A cookie is a small piece of data housed in a text file or folder on your computer's hard drive that helps us recognize you when you visit our sites. We use cookies to help us remember things such as the following: (1) the profile facts you tell us about yourself, (2) your specified areas of interest on our sites, (3) the items you've placed in your shopping basket, and/or (4) the pages you visit as you shop. The only personal information our Dress Afford cookie can contain is information that you supply yourself. Our cookies cannot read data from other parts of your computer. If you would like more technical information about how cookies work, take a look at http://www.cookiecentral.com. As stated above, you must enable cookies on your Web browser for Dress Afford to function correctly. If you are concerned about information contained in your Dress Afford cookie, you may delete the file(s) at the end of each browser session. View your browser's help file for more information on cookies. Click-stream data is information that describes the page-by-page paths you take as you browse through our websites. Using cookies, we track and store this information to help us positively customize your shopping experience. We may use click-stream data in the aggregate to provide improved services to groups of shoppers who behave similarly. We may also use it on an individual basis to improve your specific shopping experience.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Policy We understand that it is not easy to pick the perfect dress for your special day, and sometimes you might change your mind. However, all our orders, either customized items or standard items, are all made-to-order items. Once the tailoring has begun, the material cannot be recycled. The good news is that after placing your order, there is still time to change your mind. Please refer to our cancellation policy below for details. 1. Unpaid orders All unpaid orders would be cancelled automatically 30 days after you place the order. 2. Paid orders If you cancel the order before the beginning of tailoring, you are entitled to get a full refund. If you cancel the order after the beginning of tailoring, your refund is calculated upon tailoring hours. An email will be sent to inform you of the beginning of tailoring phase. In general, tailoring team will start to work within 12 hours after your payment. (learn more about our order process)
